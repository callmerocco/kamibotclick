Privacy Policy

Network Domain: kamibot.click

At KamiBot, accessible from https://kamibot.click, your privacy is a priority. This document outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it to provide our link-shortening and monetization services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized dashboard for managing your links, we collect:

  • Account Credentials: Email addresses and passwords (encrypted) or Google Profile data (UID, email, and name) via Firebase Authentication.
  • Link Metadata: Destination URLs, custom slugs, and creation timestamps.
  • Usage Statistics: Click counts and basic interaction logs for analytics.

2. Advertising & Cookies

KamiBot integrates with Adsterra and other third-party ad networks. These partners may use cookies and web beacons to serve personalized advertisements on the redirection pages.

  • Ad networks automatically receive your IP address when this occurs.
  • Cookies are used to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
  • You may opt-out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or via the Network Advertising Initiative.

3. Data Infrastructure

All user data is processed and stored using Google Firebase infrastructure. This ensures enterprise-grade security for your account and links. We do not sell your personal email data to third parties.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Users have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we store.
  • Delete their account and all associated links.
  • Request a correction of any inaccurate information.

5. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries or data deletion requests, please contact us at:
